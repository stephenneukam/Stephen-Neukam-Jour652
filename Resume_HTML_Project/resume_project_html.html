ABOUT ME

I am a reporter interested in politics, investigations and good storytelling to help shape the communities that I work in. I have experience leading a newsroom of student journalists and working collaboratively to tell the most important stories to our readers. My 2020 dive into abuse allegations at my undergraduate university in New Jersey was recognized natioanlly as the Society for Professional Journalists' top collegiate in-depth reporting series. My goal is to work in a newsroom with like-minded, motivated colleagues to do journalism that makes a difference.

EXPERIENCE
